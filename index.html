What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive land and construction solutions tailored to your needs — delivered with accuracy and professionalism.

Land Surveying

Precision boundary surveys, topographic mapping, cadastral surveys, and construction staking. We provide accurate spatial data for every project phase, ensuring legal compliance and technical reliability.

Land Sales

Verified and documented land parcels for sale. We connect buyers with legitimate, survey‑certified properties across the Western Region, handling due diligence and title verification.

Building & Construction

End‑to‑end construction support: site supervision, foundation layout, quality control, and project management for residential & commercial builds. We turn plans into reality.

Land Registration

Hassle‑free land title registration, document processing, and liaison with the Lands Commission. Secure your property rights with our expert guidance through the entire legal process.

Boundary Demarcation & Surveys

Physical demarcation of property lines using GPS and total stations. We resolve disputes, place durable beacons, and provide legally defensible boundary reports for land registration or court proceedings.

Topographic Surveys

Detailed mapping of terrain features, elevations, drainage patterns, and existing structures. Essential for architectural design, earthwork calculations, flood risk assessment, and environmental planning.

Crop / Farm / Land Compensation Surveys

Systematic assessment of crop types, tree counts, land improvements, and structures to determine fair compensation in expropriation, lease agreements, or insurance claims. Fully compliant with valuation standards.

Cadastral & Legal Surveys

Official surveys for title registration, subdivision, leasehold creation, and consent judgments. We produce plans that meet all Lands Commission requirements, helping you establish or transfer ownership with confidence.

Layout and Plot Marking

On‑site staking of building corners, road alignments, utility lines, and plot boundaries. We transfer your architectural or engineering drawings accurately to the ground, preventing costly construction errors.

AutoCAD Drawing Works

Professional 2D and 3D drafting services: site plans, floor layouts, cross‑sections, and as‑built drawings. We convert field survey data into clean, layered CAD files ready for permitting or construction.

Contour Survey

Generation of contour maps showing elevation changes at specified intervals. Critical for drainage design, cut‑and‑fill calculations, road grading, and identifying flood‑prone areas on your property.

GPS Static & GPS RTK Survey

High‑precision positioning using static GNSS for control networks and RTK for real‑time stakeout. Achieve centimetre‑level accuracy for large‑scale projects, cadastral work, and construction machine control.

About OVO SURVEYS

Based in Sekondi-Takoradi, Western Region, OVO SURVEYS delivers premium land surveying, legitimate land sales, quality building & construction, and expert land registration services.
