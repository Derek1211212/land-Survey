About OVO SURVEYS

Based in Shama, Western Region, OVO SURVEYS delivers premium land surveying, legitimate land sales, quality building & construction, and expert land registration services. We combine modern equipment (Total Stations, GNSS, drones) with decades of field experience to serve developers, government institutions, and private landowners.

Our office is located at the Shama District Assembly area, and we proudly serve the entire Western and Central Regions. Accuracy, integrity, and timely delivery are our cornerstones.
